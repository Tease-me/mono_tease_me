## Definition
TeaseMe offers influencers the ability to create AI versions of themselves that accompany and support their fans anytime. This “Influencer/OnlyFans AI Virtual Girlfriend” is an AI persona modeled on real creators, providing 24/7 human-like, personalized interactions for emotional connection and intimacy.

## Key Features
- Content defined by influencer or partner  
- Authentic persona, unique style, close to fans  
- Main use cases: chat, flirting, entertainment, emotional support  
- Enables continuous monetization for influencers (18+)

## TeaseMe Goal
Build a commercial-grade “Influencer/OnlyFans AI Virtual Girlfriend” chat service.

**MVP Stage:**
- Launch a single high-quality character with paid interaction.  
- Architecture must support future expansion into a multi-character SaaS platform with scalable and maintainable design.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Support text and voice chat  
- Ability to customize/create characters  
- Provide real-time billing, subscription, top-up functionality  
- Build profit-sharing calculation and management logic (40/60 or 50/50)  
- Scalable architecture for multi-character SaaS platform

## Expected Outcome
- **Functional:** Smooth web experience with backend supporting membership management, chat logs, billing, and profit-sharing.  
- **Business:** Ready to showcase full billing and revenue-share model to potential influencer partners, validating commercial viability.

## Project Background
AI companion chat services are rapidly growing in demand with strong global market potential.

**Entry Point Objectives:**
- Low-risk, fast validation of paid model  
- Quick cost recovery

**Long-term Vision:**
- Develop a multi-character SaaS platform for influencers/creators to launch AI avatars, creating a rich ecosystem.

**Strategic Significance:**
- Open a new business line (AI interactive entertainment / SaaS platform)  
- Build early-mover advantage  
- Develop new revenue channels  
- Foundation for team expansion

## The Problem
- Lack of attractive personalized AI personas in existing chat services  
- Influencers can’t engage fans 24/7 and lack scalable monetization tools  
- No localized “real human avatar” platform offering deep conversational experiences (chat, support, entertainment, flirting)

## Architecture Reference
- Influencer-provided video/voice data for synthesis  
- System prompts to emulate unique style and tone  
- Retrieval system or fine-tuning to strengthen persona  
- Client side: text/voice input, AI text generation, voice output  
- Admin side: membership, billing, profit-sharing management

## The Solution
**MVP Stage Outcomes:**
1. Enable character creation for influencers (“AI Virtual Girlfriend”)  
2. Offer a single influencer AI chat service with text + voice  
3. Deliver personalized conversation experiences  
4. Membership login and management  
5. Top-up and subscription flows  
6. Profit-sharing management

**Future (Post-MVP):**
- Multi-character onboarding support  
- Profit management backend / revenue reporting  
- SaaS architecture enabling “list, charge, share profit” as a service
